> Let $\Omega_\alpha$ be ==metric spaces== with Borel $\sigma$-fields $\FFF_\alpha$. Suppose we have a consistent family of finite-dimensional distributions $\{\PP_J\}$ such that $\PP_J$ is [[inner regular]] for all $J$. Then, there is a probability measure $\PP$ on the whole space $(\Omega, \FFF)$ with these finite-dimensional distributions $\PP_J$.

Quick comparison:

| | Kolmogorov | [[Ionescu-Tulcea]] |
|--- |--- | --- |
| Measurable spaces | Metric spaces + Borel fields | Anything |
| FD Marginals | Inner Regular | Product Measure |
