2. **semiclosed** under complementation; if $E\in S$, then $\Omega \setminus E$ is a finite disjoint union of elements of $S$.

> [!example] Half-open intervals
> The collection of intervals $[a,b)$ (and $(a,\infty)$) is a semialgebra.
