Let $X,X_n$ be random variables on $\RR^d$. If $\phi_{X_n}\to \phi_X$ pointwise, then $X_n$ [[Weak Convergence|converges weakly]] to $X$.

>[!idea]
>Levy's Theorem states that even if you're only given a converging sequence $\phi_{X_n}\to \phi$, with no $X$ given, if $\phi$ is continuous on a neighbourhood of $0$, then $\phi$ is the characteristic function of some random variable $X$, and $X_n\to X$ in distribution.
