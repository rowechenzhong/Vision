A ==**lie algebra**== over a field $k$ is a vector space $\fg$ over $k$ equipped with a ==**lie bracket**== $[\bullet, \bullet] : \fg \times \fg \to \fg$ which is

-   Bilinear
-   Skew-symmetric
-   Satisfies the ==**Jacobi Identity**==
    $$[[x,y],z] + [[y,z],x] + [[z,x],y] = 0$$

A ==**morphism of Lie Algebras**== is a linear map $T:\fg\to \mathfrak{h}$ preserving the Lie bracket.
