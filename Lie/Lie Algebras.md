A ==**lie algebra**== over a field $k$ is a vector space $\mathfrak{g}$ over $k$ equipped with a ==**lie bracket**== $[\bullet, \bullet] : \mathfrak{g} \times \mathfrak{g} \to \mathfrak{g}$ which is
- Bilinear
- Skew-symmetric
- Satisfies the ==**Jacobi Identity**== 
$$[[x,y],z] + [[y,z],x] + [[z,x],y] = 0$$

A ==**morphism of Lie Algebras**== is a linear map $T:\mathfrak{g}\to \mathfrak{h}$ preserving the Lie bracket.
